Main features

Advanced Noise Cancellation

Ya sea que esté en un vuelo de larga distancia o sentado en un café, los auriculares WH-1000XM4 brindan un rendimiento avanzado de cancelación de ruido, exprimiendo aún más las frecuencias medias y altas.

Superior Sound Quality

LDAC transmits approximately three times as much data (maximum transfer rate: 990 kbps) as conventional BLUETOOTH® audio, allowing you to enjoy High-Resolution Audio content with exceptional quality, similar to that of a wired connection.

Smart Listening Technology

Smart technologies to create an unhindered hands-free listening experience. For example, just speak to automatically pause music thanks to Speak-to-Chat, and with proximity and acceleration sensors, playback adapts based on whether you're wearing headphones or not.
